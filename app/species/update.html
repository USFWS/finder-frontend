Update a Species

As a species range editor your account will only allow you to update a species' range. You cannot update any other fields in the database. Please contact your at-risk species coordinator to request elevated access.

Use the pick lists below to associate an expert or office with this species. To remove an office from the list use the red trashcan button. Be sure to submit this form by pressing the update button at the bottom to ensure your updates are saved to the database.

Note: On a desktop computer, hold the Ctrl, Command, or Shift keys while clicking to select or deselect multiple options.
